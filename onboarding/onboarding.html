Typlx

Privacy-first grammar and spelling checker for any text field

1

Add your API key

Click the Typlx icon in your browser toolbar and enter your OpenAI or Anthropic API key. Your key is encrypted and never leaves your browser.

2

Write in any text field

A small purple button appears in the corner of text fields on every website.

3

Review and accept

Click the button to check your text. Typlx shows a word-by-word diff of the corrections so you can accept or reject each fix.

You can also disable Typlx on specific sites from the toolbar icon → Sites tab.
